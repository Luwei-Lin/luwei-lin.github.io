<h2>What is Next.js?</h2>
<p>Next.js is a React framework that provides:</p>
<ul>
<li>Server-side rendering (SSR)</li>
<li>Static site generation (SSG)</li>
<li>File-based routing</li>
<li>API routes</li>
<li>Built-in optimization</li>
